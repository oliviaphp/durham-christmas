Winter Balls
Here in Durham, most colleges, clubs and societies have an annual ball. Each ball has a diverse range of food and entertainment, and so dressing up for these events with your friends is the perfect way to get into the christmas spirit! Here are some of our top rated winter balls...

Law Ball


The biggest ball of the season has to be the Law Society annual ball. With beautiful table decorations, fantastic food, and awesome rides after the event, this sure is not one to miss out on!

Informal Ball (Grey College)


Informal Ball is a Ball like no other, hosted during the winter term, Grey host one of the biggest events of the year. One special twist that makes this ball spectacular is that it is fancy dress! While it might be great going to all these events in black tie, imagine having dinner dressed as a Power Ranger! This year's theme was 'Forver Young' and there were some super outfits to go with the theme...



❮ ❯


Michaelmas Ball (St Cuthberts Society)


In the Michaelmas Term, St Cuthberts Society have their annual Winter Ball, an exciting fun filled evening for all invlolved. With fantastic food, a sweet trolley, live band and much more, this is one event that leaves everybody with a festive cheer!
