Christmas Formals at the Colleges
While formals are a common event throughout the year, as Christmas approaches the colleges go that extra mile to host some spectacular christmas dinners. Tickets always sell out fast but if you're lucky enough to get a place on one of these special nights you sure will be in for a treat.

A Typical Menu

Main Course

Roast turkey Paupiette stuffed with Cumberland sausage meat cranberry and apricot, accompanied with mashed and roast potatoes, baton carrots Brussel sprouts with a rich gravy sauce

Vegetarian
